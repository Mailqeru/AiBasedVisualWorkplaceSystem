Production Realities

Environmental Limitations & Mitigations

Heavy Site Impediments

  • Visual Occlusion: Forklifts, racking pillars, and inventory blocks camera viewpoints.
  • Low Lighting / Shadowing: High dust density or weak warehouse lamps confuse standard models.
  • Processing Overhead: Concurrent stream computation demands local GPU servers.

Engineered Workarounds

  • Camera Fusion: Linking adjacent CCTV views helps models see past blindspots.
  • Equalization Filters: Contrast-enhancing preprocessing cleans dark video streams.
  • Optimized Backbones: Lightweight models (YOLO Nano) run safely on standard edge processors.
